Uranus
Uranus is the 7th planet from the Sun. Uranus is the other ice giant of the Solar System. It lacks a well defined solid surface. The atmosphere of Uranus is similar to Jupiter's and Saturn's, but it contains more ices such as water, ammonia and methane. Uranus has the coldest planetary atmosphere.
