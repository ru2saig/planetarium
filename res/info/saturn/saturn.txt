Saturn
Saturn is the ringed gas giant. Saturn primarly consists of hydrogen and helium. It lacks a definite surface, but is theorized to have a solid core. Saturn is the only planet in the Solar System that is less dense than water. The planetary rings of saturn consist of water ice, tholin and some carbon.
